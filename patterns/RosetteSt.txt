Rosette St
(Even number of sts; 4-rnd repeat)
Work Rosette: P2tog, but do not drop sts from left-hand needle, k2tog same 2 sts, slip both sts from left-hand needle together.
Note: Pattern results in a spiral and beginning of rnd marker moves forward 1 st every other rnd
Rnd 1: Knit
Rnd 2: *Work Rosette; repeat from * to end of pattern section.
Rnd 3: Knit
^Rnd 4: Slip 1, *work rosette; repeat from * to end of pattern section. [perform the last rosette repeat with the first K st of following St st section]
